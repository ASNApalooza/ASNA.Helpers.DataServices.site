What is Walter?

In the Big Lebowski, Walter Sobchak was The Dude's faithful sidekick. This library is intended to be a faithful sidekick for your AVR projects. The name Walter is also a nod to one of ASNA's resident geniuses, Walter Goodwin. Walter's quiet, steady presence has had a huge influence on all that we do with AVR for many years.

Let's see what Walter can do for you.
